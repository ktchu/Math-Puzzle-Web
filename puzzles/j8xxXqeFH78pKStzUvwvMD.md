## Triangle Inequalities - Hanging Out Inside

Let point $P$ lie in the interior of $\triangle ABC$.

![Triangle](figures/j8xxXqeFH78pKStzUvwvMD.png)

Show that

$$
\frac{1}{2} (AB + BC + AC) \le AP + BP + CP \le AB + BC + AC
$$
