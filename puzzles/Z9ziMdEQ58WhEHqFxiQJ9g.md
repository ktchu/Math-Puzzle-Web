## Triangle Inequalities - Sitting on Edge

Let point $D$ lies on side $BC$ of triangle.

![Triangle](figures/Z9ziMdEQ58WhEHqFxiQJ9g.png)

Prove that

$$
\frac{1}{2} (AB + AC) - \frac{1}{2} BC
\le AD
\le \frac{1}{2} (AB + AC) + \frac{1}{2} BC
$$
