## Friends of $\left( x + \frac{1}{x} \right)$

Suppose that we know that $\left( x + \frac{1}{x} \right) = 3$. Without solving for $x$,
calculate the following quantities:

* $x^2 + \frac{1}{x^2}$

* $x^3 + \frac{1}{x^3}$

* $x^4 + \frac{1}{x^4}$

* $x^5 + \frac{1}{x^5}$
